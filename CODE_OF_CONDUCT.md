# Code of Conduct (Internal)

## 1. Purpose

We are committed to maintaining a professional, respectful, and productive environment for collaboration.

## 2. Expected Conduct

Participants are expected to:

- Communicate respectfully and constructively
- Provide feedback focused on the work product, not the individual
- Be considerate of differing experience levels and perspectives
- Maintain clear written records of technical decisions where appropriate

## 3. Prohibited Conduct

The following behavior is not acceptable:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct
- Personal attacks, intimidation, or threats
- Publication of private, confidential, or sensitive information (including credentials)
- Deliberate disruption of reviews, discussions, or team processes

## 4. Scope

This applies to participation in:

- Issues, Pull Requests, code reviews, discussions, and documentation in this repository
- Related internal communication channels when discussing work in this repository

## 5. Reporting

Report concerns to:

- People Ops / HR: `people-ops@TUEMPRESA.com`
- Security (if relevant): `security@TUEMPRESA.com`

Reports will be handled confidentially to the extent feasible.

## 6. Enforcement

Maintainers and the company may take appropriate action, including:

- Requesting corrective behavior
- Limiting participation
- Revoking access
- Escalation to People Ops/HR per internal policy
